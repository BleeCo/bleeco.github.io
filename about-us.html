Questions

  • Where can I buy Blee Watch?

    Blee watch is almost ready to meet you. We will be launching our Commercial Pilot (Test Run) in the month of May 2017. After the Test Run Blee Watch will be available for purchase. Don't forget to subscribe to us to know every update.

    If you want to be a part of our Pilot, please write to us on janhavi@getblee.com and we will get back to you about the same.

  • Can you explain 'How does Blee work' in Sign Language?

    Sure. We would love to! Just send us an email at nupura@getblee.com or janhavi@getblee.com and we will arrange a video call for you in no time. In fact, if you have any other queries regarding Blee, we are happy to answer. If necessary, we can  arrange a demo as well.

  • I am partially Deaf. Is Blee useful for me?

    Blee uses sense of touch and vision to notify you about variety of audio alerts. As it is not dependent on sense of hearing, anyone can get benefits of Blee ranging from the profound deaf person to a hearing person. From our research we have identified that Blee is also very useful for Senior Citizens who are losing sense of hearing because of their age.

    To know more about technology used, please write to nupura@getblee.com and we will answer all your queries.

  • Do you accept donations?

    Thank you so much for your interest and support. We would love to have you involved in our activities and your donation is very valuable for us. However BleeTech Innovation as a company do not accept donations for operations and development. But you can donate our Blee Watch(es) to an Individual or an Institution. If you are interested, please let us know at janhavi@getblee.com and we will share further details about the recipients.

  • Are there any Job opportunities at Blee?

    Big YES! We are looking for passionate and talented people to join hands with us. If you think BleeTech Innovations is the right place for you don't hesitate to write to us at nupura@getblee.com . At BleeTech everyday is a brand new challenge and we take pride in learning something new each day.  Working from a busy startup hub gives you daily dose of excitement  and the much needed exposure if you are a fresh graduate. Join team Blee and share this excitement of working in the iconic BSE Building in the heart of Mumbai City.`
