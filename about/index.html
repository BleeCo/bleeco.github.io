About Us

Nupura and Janhavi had an idea to build something which deaf performers could use to synchronize their dance act. After brainstorming, they came up with an exciting design, and then decided to join forces with Ninad to bring it out into reality.


The 3 of them have won quite a few accolades for their work on Blee : prominent among them, a GYTI Award for a Socially relevant Innovation at Rashtrapati Bhavan. Blee was initially called Sway
