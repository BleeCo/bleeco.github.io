FAQs

What's the range for Blee ?

Around 30 metres.

Can I connect to multiple Blee devices at the same time ?

Yes you can ! Blee works over WiFi and our app can connect to multiple devices at the same time.

What is the battery life?

3 hours on continuous maximum operation. On an average you can expect to get 5 hour operation on full charge.
