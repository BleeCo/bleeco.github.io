Recognitions

Blee is a digital platform for deaf and hard of hearing individuals which helps them use wearable tech for making lives simpler.

There are several application for which Blee provides you support if you are deaf. Some of the important are listed below.

 

Calling out : You don’t realize when your friend is calling you unless you see him or he comes and taps you. Blee can recognizing when your name is being called and notify you.

 

Traffic feedback : Have a safer drive with Blee telling you about the honking happening around you.

 

Music : Experience the music through touch. Dance around with it. Dance is always the best way for you to express!

 

 

Deaf children are very focused when they learn new things. It would be very interesting to see Blee being used by them for speech therapy and other applications.

Team Blee

Blee is a product conceived by Janhavi and Nupura, two passionate industrial designers.
They believe that technology and design innovation should benefit the people facing real problems. With academic background in product design, they specialize in user research through interactions and observations.
It is very important to understand and experience the problem in some way for reaching to the perfect solution. They always love to see there design solutions making lives simpler and better.
