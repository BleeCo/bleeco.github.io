Meet blee


Blee is a wearable device that lets you synchronize with music, through vibrations. Musicians can use Blee as a vibration-based metronome, to keep up with the beat. Multiple Blee devices can be connected to a smartphone, and controlled simultaneously, to maintain synchronicity among musicians.
Dancers too, can use Blee to maintain count! Dance is a great medium of expression for people with hearing impairments. Owing to these physical limitations, deaf dancers find it difficult to sync with their peers. Blee solves this problem by enabling them to maintain a song's beat count, and groove gracefully to the music.

FAQs


What's the range for a Blee device?

Around 30 metres.


Can I connect to multiple Blee devices at the same time ?

Yes you can ! Blee works over WiFi and our app can connect to multiple devices at the same time.


Can I make a vibration pattern as per my taste?

Yes you can ! We have provided a tap to create feature, just for this purpose. You can play a song in the background in our app, and tap at times where you wish to have beats. The app stores the information and transfers it to the device.


Can I log my practice timings online?

Yes you can ! BleeCloud will track your practice times, along with many other parameters which help you track your progress with the instrument.


Can I share my vibration pattern with my bandmates/ friends?

Of course you can! We provide you cloud space when you buy a Blee device and link it to your Blee account. You can share a .vib pattern with your friend, so that he/she can have the same experience as you. This works particularly well when you and your band want to share a .vib pattern with each other.
