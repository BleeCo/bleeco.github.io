What is Blee?

A stylish new SmartWatch designed especially for Deaf individuals! One watch for all your needs. Blee can listen to your environment and notify you about all the sound alerts with vibrations, colors and icons. It can also speak out words or sentences for you. You can also dance to any rhythm with Blee. And, It also helps you stay connected with your loved ones in case of emergency!

Notify

Are you missing out on many sounds everyday?

We understand that sounds like Doorbell, Alarm Clock seem very basic, but affect your mobility and independence. That's why Blee notifies you about all these sounds with vibrations and icons.

Do you find it difficult to start

a conversation with anyone?

Use our text to speech feature and you will never have to search for words to start a conversation. Use our template sentences or save your own. Save names and call out the person anytime you want with Blee. Blee can shout out for help if you need.

Dance

Tried of looking at somebody for clues?

Your own counts, your own music! Feel it, express it. Be an independent performer! Discrete vibration counts just for you.

Stay Connected with your loved ones,

with a click of a button!

Save up to 5 Emergency Contacts and you will never be alone. In case of emergency, just press the panic switch and we will contact all your loved ones with your location!
