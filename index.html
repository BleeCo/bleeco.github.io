Meet blee


Blee is a wearable device that connects to your smartphone, and lets you synchronize with your music, through vibrations. Any task that requires synchronicity, will find Blee useful. Musicians can use this to set a vibration-based metronome, so that the sound of the metronome does not interfere with their music. While playing in a group, multiple Blee devices can be controlled through one smartphone.
Dancers can use this to maintain count too! Dance is a great medium of expression for people with hearing impairments. However, owing to their physical limitations, they face several problems.
Blee adds a new dimension to music by giving a unique sensorial experience.
