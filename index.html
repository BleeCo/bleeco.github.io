MEET BLEE

Blee is a wearable band for deaf and hard of hearing individuals. In everyday life, deaf people miss out on many events, sometimes even emergency notifications just because they cannot react to sound. Blee converts sound into vibrations and notifies them about the event, its direction, proximity etc.

With higher costs and several other reasons, hearing aids’ penetration is extremely low around the world; which is less than 10%. Blee is the perfect affordable solution for the deaf community including a large number of elderly population; alarming them about the most essential sounds that one cannot afford to miss.

RECOGNITIONS

 QUESTIONS ?

  • What is Blee?

    Blee is a wearable device which is connected to the smartphone with Bluetooth. It gives sound alerts in the form of vibrations. Blee is especially helpful for people who suffer from hearing loss. User can record any sound around him/her with the smartphone and get vibration notifications of the sound samples on the wearable. Wearable supports vibration alerts along with light alerts.

  • What all sounds can be converted to vibrations with Blee?

    Emergency sounds like Fire alarms are pre-recorded. Additionally user can record any sound of his/her choice and assign a vibration pattern and/or lights to it. These sound samples could be your door bell, landline phone, mobile phone, alarm clock, even cry of your baby and the name by which people call you!

  • Where can I buy the device?

    We are making Blee better for you. In next 6 months Blee will be available for sales on this same website. Meanwhile stay connected to know every update about Blee by signing up for our newsletter. You can also pre-order Blee from our crowdfunding campaign which is coming soon.

  • I am partially deaf. Is Blee useful for me ?

    Blee uses sense of touch and vision to notify you about variety of audio alerts. As it is not dependent on sense of hearing anyone can get benefits of Blee ranging from the profound deaf person to the hearing person.
