Meet blee


Blee is a wearable device that lets you synchronize with your music, through vibrations. Dance is a great medium of expression for people with hearing impairments. However, owing to their physical limitations, they face several problems. Blee adds a new dimension to music by giving a unique sensorial experience. It aims to make these artists completely independent.

The product essentially has two parts, a wearable ring and a smart-phone application. Any song is converted into vibrations through the software which are then transferred to the ring via wireless mode of communication.

Blee not only helps the deaf feel music, but it could also be a companion assisting them in different tasks throughout the day by giving tactile feedback.
